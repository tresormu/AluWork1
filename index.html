Why We Exist

Our Mission

Our mission is to solve Uganda's youth unemployment crisis — because job creation is not just an economic issue, it is a matter of national survival. Through bold research, community-driven advocacy, and innovative solutions, we work to create real jobs, restore dignity, and build the stable future Uganda's young people deserve.

The Challenge

Problem Statement

In Kiruhura, Uganda, thousands of young people wake up every day with energy and dreams — but nowhere to direct them. Nationally, 16.1% of young Ugandans are unemployed and 41% of youth aged 18–30 are not in employment, education, or training. Young people are not lazy — they are trapped. Our Think Tank exists to change that.
